📐 Key Formula

Read → Find key words → Write equation → Solve → Check

💡 Example

"8 apples, gave 3 away, how many left?" → 8 - 3 = 5 apples

🧠 Memory Anchor (Mnemonic)

💡 Tip: Key words: "total/altogether" → add; "left/remain" → subtract; "each × groups" → multiply.

The essence of Basic Word Problems lies in understanding the underlying mathematical structure. Try to:

⚠️ Common Errors

Watch out for these common mistakes when working with basic word problems:

  • Confusing related but different concepts
  • Forgetting to check units and verify answers
  • Rushing through steps without showing work

Always verify your answer by substituting back into the original problem!
