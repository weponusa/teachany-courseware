🔍 Level 1: Find the Main Idea

阅读文章，回答主旨与细节问题

+80 XP
📰 The Amazing Honeybee

Honeybees are fascinating creatures. They live in large groups called colonies, which can have up to 60,…

Honey is not just delicious — it is also very useful. People have used honey as medicine for thousands of years. Scientists have found that honey can kill bacteria and help heal wounds. Honey never spoils; archaeologists found 3,000-year-old honey in Egyptian tombs that was still good to eat!

Unfortunately, honeybees are in danger. Many bees are dying because of pesticides, loss of habitat, and disease. Without bees, many plants could not produce fruit or seeds. We must protect these amazing insects.

1. What is the MAIN IDEA of this passage?

2. According to the passage, what do WORKER BEES do?

3. Why does the author mention "3,000-year-old honey in Egyptian tombs"?

⚠️ 易错点提醒
  • 🔴易错：b 和 d 镜像混淆：书写时将 b 写成 d 或反之：注意规范书写和理解概念
  • 🔴易错：字母名称音和字母发音混淆：认为字母在单词中的发音就是字母名称音：注意规范书写和理解概念
  • 🔴易错：短元音和长元音混读：在闭音节中把元音读成长元音：注意规范书写和理解概念
