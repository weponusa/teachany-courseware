🔤 Power Words — 点击展开例句

instead of "good"
Use: excellent, superb, outstanding, remarkable
"She gave an outstanding performance" ✨
instead of "bad"
Use: dreadful, terrible, appalling, disastrous
"The weather was dreadful that day." 🌧️
instead of "said"
Use: declared, whispered, insisted, explained
"\"Help!\" she whispered in the dark." 😮
instead of "went"
Use: dashed, strolled, rushed, crept, wandered
"He dashed towards the finish line." 🏃
instead of "big"
Use: enormous, massive, colossal, vast, towering
"A colossal wave swept the beach." 🌊
instead of "very"
Use: extremely, incredibly, remarkably, utterly
"It was incredibly cold that morning." ❄️

+140 XP

📐 Paragraph Structure Guide:

🎣 Step 1: Hook + Topic Sentence

以引人入胜的开头句抓住读者，然后说明这段的主题

💡 Step 2: Supporting Details (2-3 sentences)

用具体事实、例子或原因来支撑你的主题句

✅ Step 3: Conclusion Sentence

用一句话总结本段，或给读者一个思考/行动的呼吁
