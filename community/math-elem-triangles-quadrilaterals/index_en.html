📐 Key Formula

Triangle angle sum = 180° | Quadrilateral angle sum = 360°

💡 Example

Isosceles triangle with two 60° angles → third angle = 180°-60°-60° = 60° (equilateral!)

🧠 Memory Anchor (Mnemonic)

💡 Tip: The three sides of a triangle must satisfy: sum of any two > third side!
