¶1 Yesterday, our class went on a special trip to the zoo. We woke up early and boarded the school bus. Everyone was very excited.
¶2 At the zoo, we saw many animals. The elephants were huge and friendly. The monkeys were funny and made us laugh. There was also a beautiful peacock with colourful feathers.
¶3 Our teacher, Miss Lin, told us many interesting facts about the animals. We learned that elephants can remember things for a very long time. That was amazing!
¶4 After lunch, we visited the aquarium. We saw sharks, turtles, and many colourful fish. It was breathtaking!
¶5 We returned to school in the afternoon. Everyone was tired but happy. It was one of the best days of our school year. I hope we can go again next year!

📚 高级阅读策略
01
SQ3R法
Survey(浏览)→Question(提问)→Read(阅读)→Recite(复述)→Review(回顾)，系统提升阅读理解力。
02
主题句定位
每段第一句通常是主题句，快速把握段落大意。遇到问题时，先定位相关段落。
03
关键词高亮
时间词(yesterday/first)、地点词(at the zoo)、人物名词是定位答案的关键线索。
04
排除法
选择题中，先排除明显错误的选项，缩小范围，再从剩余选项中选最佳答案。
🎁 Bonus 加练短文：Our School Garden
Our school has a beautiful garden. There are many flowers and trees. In spring, the flowers are red, yellow, and pink. Students often come here to read books and relax. We take care of the garden every week. Our teacher says a beautiful environment makes us happy and helps us learn better.

⚠️ 易错点提醒
  • 🔴易错：b 和 d 镜像混淆：书写时将 b 写成 d 或反之：注意规范书写和理解概念
  • 🔴易错：字母名称音和字母发音混淆：认为字母在单词中的发音就是字母名称音：注意规范书写和理解概念
  • 🔴易错：短元音和长元音混读：在闭音节中把元音读成长元音：注意规范书写和理解概念

💡 常见错误往往就在细节中，仔细检查每一步！

📚 BLOOM'S TAXONOMY EXERCISES
REMEMBER

UNDERSTAND

APPLY

ANALYZE → CREATE
