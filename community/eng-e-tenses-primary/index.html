📌 四种时态句型对比
时态 过去时 现在时 进行时 将来时
肯定 She played. She plays. She is playing. She will play.
否定 She didn't play. She doesn't play. She isn't playing. She won't play.
疑问 Did she play? Does she play? Is she playing? Will she play?
